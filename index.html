Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Automate your business processes with RPA for increased efficiency

What is RPA?

Robotic process automation (RPA) is a form of business process automation that is based on software robots (bots) or artificial intelligence (AI) agents.[1] It is sometimes referred to as software robotics (not to be confused with robot software)is a technology that uses software robots to automate repetitive tasks and processes in business operations. RPA bots can perform tasks such as data entry, data extraction, form filling, and more, freeing up human employees to focus on more strategic and value-added activities.

How RPA Works

RPA software robots are trained to mimic the actions of human users by interacting with applications and systems through the user interface. They can work 24/7 and are highly adaptable, making them ideal for various industries and tasks.
